# BI-MONTHLY BUILD CHALLENGE: OLYMPIC STADIUM!

半月建筑赏: 奥林匹克体育场

****

是时候迎来我们下一个半月建筑挑战了，本次的主题是: 奥林匹克体育场！我十分热衷于观看今年在东京举办的大型比赛，所以我非常希望在本次建筑挑战中让他能再存在一段时间。
It’s time for another bi-monthly build challenge, and the category is Olympic Stadium! I loved watching the big games in Tokyo this year, so much that I wanted to keep it going just a little longer with this build challenge.

本次挑战的主题要求参与者们自己建造一个拥有适当大小的体育场，并且场地内要有奥运火炬。 这个主题也因充满乐趣而备受欢迎。
The challenge theme asked participants to build a stadium of a decent size by themselves, and to include the Olympic Torch. It was also very much encouraged to have fun.

## 第1名: SkyBlue2929

SkyBlue2929 的这张地图的范围、大小以及许多细节令人印象深刻。 您可以看到看台上的奥运五环标志，以 V 字形在上空飞行的喷气式飞机队列，以及一条我都想绕着跑一圈的跑道 —— 尽管我并不能保证我能跟上那些人！
The scope, size, and detail of this submission from SkyBlue2929 is impressive. You can see the Olympic Rings as a pattern in the stands, jets flying above in V formation, and a track even I would want to take a lap around – although no guarantees I’d be able to keep up!

![](pic\20210911\olynpic1.webp)

## 第2名: aelonelo

看看这个由 aelonelo 建造的华丽的体育场！ 当你第一次看到这个时，它一定会让你叹为观止。 体育场外观的细节只是个开始，因为体育场内人群的细致外观会令人惊讶！ 它看起来如此逼真，以至于我不得不再看一眼。
Check out this gorgeous stadium from aelonelo! When seeing this for the first time, it really does take your breath away. The details on the exterior of the stadium are just the beginning as there is the surprisingly detailed appearance of crowds in the stadium! It looks so realistic I had to take a second glance.
![](pic\20210911\olympic2.webp)

## 第3名: astros

astros 的地图给我们带来了一些独特的夜空、背景中的一些城市的轮廓，以及真正吸引人的华丽灯光。 在体育场内部，那些微妙的灯光看起来十分温暖，吸引着我们的眼球。
This submission by astros gave us something unique with the night sky, bit of city skyline in the background, and gorgeous lighting that really caught our eye. The subtle glow inside the stadium looks really warm and inviting.
